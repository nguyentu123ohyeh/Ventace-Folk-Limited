About Us

Empowering modern living with smart technology and global vision.

Ventace Folk Limited

Ventace Folk Limited is an international import-export company based in Singapore, specializing in smart technology, electronics, and accessories for global business partners. Since our establishment in 2021, we have aimed to become a reliable bridge between the world’s leading technology brands and distributors, wholesalers, and project partners worldwide.

Our Vision & Mission

We believe that advanced technology drives sustainable growth and enhances the operational efficiency of businesses. Our vision is to be a trusted import-export partner, delivering the most innovative products and solutions to the global market.

Our mission is to optimize the supply chain by providing comprehensive logistics, technical consulting, legal support, and after-sales services for corporate clients, distributors, and large-scale projects.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Import-Export Expertise: Deep knowledge of international customs procedures and logistics, ensuring your goods arrive on time and in full compliance.
  • Diverse Product Portfolio: Carefully selected smart home devices and technology accessories from reputable global manufacturers.
  • Flexible Partnerships: Direct collaboration with factories, distributors, and wholesalers, including OEM/ODM support for project needs.
  • End-to-End Solutions: Technical consulting, documentation support, cargo insurance, and professional after-sales service for businesses.

Why Choose Ventace Folk Limited?

  • Global Network: Connections across Asia, Europe, and the Americas, with multilingual and multicultural support.
  • Optimized Solutions: Flexible shipping, payment methods, and scalable order fulfillment for any project size.
  • Transparency & Security: Commitment to partner confidentiality and transparent transactions.
  • Growth Partnership: Supporting your business to expand into new markets and strengthen your global brand.

Our Future Direction

Ventace Folk Limited is committed to expanding our international import-export network and continuously improving our service quality. We are ready to accompany your business on the journey to global integration and sustainable growth.

Ventace Folk Limited – Your trusted import-export partner. Connecting technology. Reaching the world.
