Return Policy

We want you to be 100% satisfied with your purchase. Our return policy is designed to be simple, fair, and transparent.

Eligibility for Returns
  • Returns are accepted within 14 days of receiving your order.
  • Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in the same condition as received.
  • Customized, clearance, or final sale items are not eligible for return unless defective.
How to Return
  1. 1 Contact Us: Email info@ventacefolkworld.com with your order number and reason for return.
  2. 2 Pack Your Item: Ensure the product is unused and securely packaged.
  3. 3 Ship Back: We will provide you with return instructions and address.
  4. 4 Refund: Once received and inspected, we will process your refund within 3-7 business days.
Important Notes
  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.
  • Approved refunds will be made to your original payment method.
  • For exchanges, please mention your replacement request in your email.
Need Help?
For any questions or urgent support, please email info@ventacefolkworld.com.
